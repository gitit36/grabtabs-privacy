GrabTabs Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Overview

GrabTabs is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how GrabTabs handles your data.

Data Collection

GrabTabs does not collect, store, or transmit any user data to external servers.

Local Storage

GrabTabs stores the following information locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API:

  • User Preferences: Default output mode (Simple/Smart), default export format, and dark mode preference
  • AI API Key: If you choose to use Smart mode, your AI provider API key is stored locally on your device

All stored data remains on your device and is never transmitted to GrabTabs servers or any third-party servers.

AI Provider API Calls

When you enable Smart mode and provide an AI API key, GrabTabs makes API calls directly to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, or Grok). These calls:

  • Are made directly from your browser to the AI provider's API
  • Include only the tab titles and URLs you select to copy
  • Are subject to the privacy policy of your chosen AI provider
  • Are never routed through GrabTabs servers

GrabTabs does not have access to, store, or log any data sent to AI providers.

Tab Information

GrabTabs accesses your browser tabs only to:

  • Read tab titles and URLs when you explicitly click "Copy Tabs"
  • Format this information for copying to your clipboard
  • Process tab data locally in your browser

Tab information is never transmitted anywhere except when you explicitly use Smart mode (in which case it goes directly to your chosen AI provider).

No Analytics or Tracking

GrabTabs does not:

  • Collect analytics or usage statistics
  • Use tracking cookies or pixels
  • Send data to third-party analytics services
  • Monitor your browsing behavior

Data Sharing

GrabTabs does not share any data with third parties. The only exception is when you use Smart mode, in which case tab data is sent directly to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, or Grok) using your own API key.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • You can delete all stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • You can clear your preferences by resetting the extension settings
  • Your API keys can be removed at any time through the settings page

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact support@onp0int.com.

Summary: GrabTabs stores your preferences and API key locally on your device. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared. Your privacy is protected.
